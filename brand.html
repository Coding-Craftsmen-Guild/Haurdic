01 — IDENTITY

The mark & lockup.

A circular H monogram paired with the Playfair wordmark and a tracked-out descriptor. The monogram may stand alone; the wordmark should always sit with the descriptor beneath it.

02 — COLOUR

A warm, earthen palette.

Every colour is a token, defined once as an RGB channel triplet in :root and consumed as a Tailwind utility. Opacity modifiers (/90, /40) work everywhere.

HOW TOKENS ARE DEFINED
:root {
  --c-cream:  249 246 240;   /* page background */
  --c-ink:     44  37  34;   /* primary text / dark surfaces */
  --c-brass:  197 160 111;   /* gold accent */
}
// tailwind.config
colors: { ink: 'rgb(var(--c-ink) / <alpha-value>)' }
// usage  bg-ink  text-brass  border-line/40

06 — VOICE & TONE

How Haurdić speaks.

Warm, assured, and specific. We sound like a third-generation maker who knows the craft — never like a marketing department. Every line ships in English and Croatian with equal care.

01

Heritage, not hype

Lead with craft and time. “Three generations of mastery,” not “world-class solutions.”

02

Concrete & precise

Real numbers earn trust: 58 years, 13 artisans, 42 pieces in six weeks.

03

Personal & warm

First person, human promises. “We treat every commission as a personal promise.”

04

Bilingual parity

EN and HR are equals. Croatian keeps the same warmth — never a literal machine echo.
